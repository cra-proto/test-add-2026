Personal income tax

Canada workers benefit (CWB) – Personal income tax

  • Federal: Line 45300
  • Tax year: 2025| Prior tax years: 2024 and earlier

The Canada Workers Benefit (CWB) is a refundable tax credit to help individuals and families who are working and earning a low income.

The CWB has two parts: a basic amount and a disability supplement.

Sections

Who is eligible
Depending on your income and other circumstances, you may be eligible for the Canada Workers Benefit.
How much you can get
The amount you can expect to receive for the Canada Workers Benefit depends on your income and your province or territory of residence.
How to claim
You can claim the Canada Workers Benefit when you file your tax return.
